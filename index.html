Bill Gates

Biodata

  • Full Name: William Henry Gates III
  • Born: October 28, 1955
  • Birthplace: Seattle, Washington, USA

  • Net Worth: Over $100 billion (as of recent estimates)

Bill Gates is one of the world's most influential people in technology and philanthropy. As the co-founder of Microsoft, he revolutionized personal computing. Along with his philanthropic efforts, he has made significant contributions to global health and education through the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation.
